📊 CVM Underpenetrated Segments Analysis (Syntegra)

🔍 Project Overview

This project analyzes Syntegra-generated cardiovascular (CVM) claims data to identify underpenetrated patient–provider segments where market growth is not aligned with provider engagement.

The goal is to uncover high-opportunity segments and translate data into actionable commercial strategies for sales, marketing, and market access teams.

🎯 Business Objective

Healthcare commercial teams often face a gap between growing patient demand and provider-level engagement.

This project aims to:

Identify high-growth CVM segments (by age group and HCP tier)
Detect under-engaged providers in those segments
Enable data-driven sales force deployment and targeting strategies
⚙️ Approach & Methodology
Data Preparation: Cleaned and validated claims data using Python
Data Quality Checks: Performed null checks, uniqueness validation, and consistency checks post-transformations
Segmentation:
Patient segmentation by age groups
Provider segmentation by HCP tiers
Analysis:
Growth trends across segments (YoY analysis)
Claims distribution across HCP tiers
Identification of mismatch between demand and engagement
📈 Key Insights
Certain mid-tier HCP segments show strong patient growth but lower engagement
Specific age groups (e.g., aging population) drive disproportionate claim volume growth
Opportunities exist to realign commercial efforts toward high-growth but underserved segments
💡 Business Impact (For Leadership)

This analysis helps leadership:

📌 Optimize sales force allocation toward high-impact provider segments

📌 Improve targeting of physicians and accounts based on growth signals

📌 Drive better ROI on commercial efforts through focused engagement

📌 Support strategic decision-making with data-backed insights


🛠️ Tools & Technologies

Python (Pandas, NumPy)

Jupyter Notebook

Data Visualization (Matplotlib/Seaborn)


🚀 Outcome

A structured analytics framework that bridges data → insights → strategy, enabling healthcare organizations to act on hidden growth opportunities in the CVM landscape.
